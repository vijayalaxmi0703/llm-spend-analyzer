# Economics

## Funnel assumptions
- 1,200 monthly visitors from launch channels
- 20% of visitors start an audit
- 40% of audits result in a captured lead
- 25% of leads convert to a follow-up conversation

## CAC estimate
- Paid acquisition not assumed for launch.
- Organic channel value is high for founder-first SaaS.
- If paid ads are used later, a conservative CAC target is $120 per qualified founder.

## Revenue model
- Immediate MVP is lead generation for Credex consulting.
- Future product revenue can come from premium audit subscriptions and recurring optimization alerts.

## ARR math
Assuming 35 qualified leads in month 1 and a 10% consulting conversion at $4,500 average engagement:

- 3 consulting conversions → $13,500 initial revenue
- If retained at 20% monthly recurring audit monitoring, ARR potential grows quickly.

## Profitability assumptions
- Infrastructure costs are low with Next.js and Supabase.
- The product is designed as a lead-gen funnel, so gross margins are expected to be high once initial traffic is acquired.
